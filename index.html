About Me

I'm an extroverted, passionate software developer and abstract artist who loves people, painting and coding! As a junior dev, I am most passionate about painting a canvas with the magical tools of HTML, CSS and JavaScript all working together to create beautiful art rendered on a website. My first spark of interest in becoming a software developer started in UI/UX design, first as a fashion blogger and later as I created a website and content for myself, clients and my class websites as a French Instructor. After a decade of teaching language and a year transitioning into art full-time, I was looking for a new career path to marry all my creative interests and channel the elements that make me thrive: creation, ideation, problem-solving, challenge, mentorship and teamwork. After witnessing the life-changing career paths of friends who enrolled in Nashville Software School, I decided to commit and enroll in the 6 month full-stack development bootcamp. In learning to be a software developer, I am able to employ the beginner’s mind that I cultivated as an adult language learner and teacher, my creativity as an artist, and my passion for people and interaction through team projects and the mentorship of mid and senior software developers..
